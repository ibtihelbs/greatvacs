Most vacuums today are made in China — low suction, flimsy parts, designed to be replaced every few years. Kirby and Rainbow are built differently. They're made in the USA with metal components and real engineering.

These machines last decades when properly rebuilt. Rebuilding isn't cutting corners. It's a smarter long-term decision — you get the same American-made quality at roughly half the price.
